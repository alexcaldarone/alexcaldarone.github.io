This is an [R shiny app](https://alexcaldarone.shinyapps.io/commodity_prices/) to view commodity prices.

The "RICI Indexes" section shows all the Rogers International Commodity Indexes (RICI, RICI-Agriculture, RICI-Energy, RICI-Metals) and shows their performance relative to the S&P 500.
The section "Commodity Prices" shows the price of various commodities. It is made up of several pages. The "Price plotting" page plots the price of the selected commodity. The pages Metals, Oil & Gas, Farm & Fishery, Agriculture softs, Fruits & Nusts show the price tables of the commodities in each section. Every table is made up of commodity, price, date, YTD price change and percentage off all time high.

Indexes: RICI, RICIA, RICIE, RICIM.

Metals: Gold, Silver, Platinum, Palladium, Aluminum, Copper, Iron Ore, Lead, Nickel, Zinc, Tin, Uranium.

Oil & Gas: WTI Oil, Numex Oil, Brent Oil, Gas (Nymex), Gas (ICE).

Grains: Corn, Oats, Rice, Soybeans, Wheat.

Farm & Fishery: Live cattle, Poultry, Pork, Salmon, Shrimp, Dairy, Wool.

Agriculture softs: Arabica coffee, Robusta coffee, Coffee (futures), Cocoa beans, Cocoa (futures), Tea, Sugar.

Fruit & Nuts: Peanuts, Oranges, Orange Juice, Bananas.

All data comes from Quandl.

To view the dashboard click [here](https://alexcaldarone.shinyapps.io/commodity_prices/)
