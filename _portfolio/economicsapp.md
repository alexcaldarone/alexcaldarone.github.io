A [R shiny app](https://alexcaldarone.shinyapps.io/economicsapp/) to visualize and track major stock markets, economic data for the US and EU, gold and silver markets and various financial market ratios.

USA stock markets: Dow Jones, S&P 500, Nasdaq. 

EU stock markets: Euronext, FTSEMIB (Italy), DAX 30 (Germany), CAC 40 (France), FTSE 100 (UK), IBEX (Spain), AEX (Netherlands). 

Asian stock markets: Nikkei (Japan), Hang Seng (Hong Kong), SSE (China).

USA economic data: Debt-to-GDP, M2, M2 velocity, FED balance sheet, Fed Funds rate, 10 yr yield minus 2 yr yield, 10 yr treasury rate, 1 yr treasury rate, Total credit market debt obligations, Total credit market debt-to-GDP. 

EU economic data: EU inflation yoy, EU cpi, EU debt-to-GDP, EU M3 money supply, EU 1 yr bond yield, Eu 10 yr bond yield.

Gold & Silver markets: Gold & silver prices, Gold & silver prices (EUR), gold-to-silver ratio, dow-gold ratio, dow-silver ratio, gold-sp ratio, sp-gold ratio, real estate to gold, real estate to silver. 

Financial market ratios: Shiller PE ratio (S&P 500), marketcap-to-GDP, nasdaq-to-sp, gdx-to-qqq, S&P 500 dividend yield.


To view the dashboard click [here](https://alexcaldarone.shinyapps.io/economicsapp/).
